Artist statement

Hunar-e-Hajar is a practice of slow looking—building atmosphere through layered pigments, light, and detail. Each piece begins with a feeling, then becomes a place to return to.

The collection moves between still life, landscapes, nature studies, and heritage-inspired architecture—each category a different kind of calm.

“I paint what I want to live inside—soft edges, honest color, and stories that don’t rush.”

— Hunar-e-Hajar
